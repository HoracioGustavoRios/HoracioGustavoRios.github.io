Quantto

Quantto is a simple yet powerful tool for counting your inventory. With our application, you can quickly and accurately count your inventory, making it easier to manage your stock levels and replenish when necessary. Our easy-to-use interface allows you to easily upload your available inventory, assign inventory managers, and start counting your products simply by scanning barcodes with your phone's camera. Finish faster, with fewer errors, and fewer personnel.
