Web App

Quantto is a cloud-based inventory management software designed for small and medium-sized businesses. It provides real-time tracking of inventory levels, sales, and purchasing activities. Quantto allows users to manage their inventory from anywhere with an internet connection, providing access to inventory data on the go. With Quantto, users can easily create and manage purchase orders, sales orders, and invoices. The software automatically updates inventory levels as products are sold, received, or returned. This helps businesses stay on top of inventory levels and avoid stockouts or overstocking. Quantto also provides analytics and reporting features, enabling users to generate reports on inventory levels, sales trends, and purchasing activities. This helps businesses make informed decisions about inventory management and purchasing. One unique feature of Quantto is its integration with major eCommerce platforms, including Shopify, Amazon, and WooCommerce. This enables users to manage their inventory across multiple sales channels from a single platform, reducing the risk of overselling or stockouts. In summary, Quantto is a comprehensive inventory management software that helps small and medium-sized businesses stay on top of their inventory levels, sales, and purchasing activities. With its cloud-based platform, real-time tracking, and analytics features, Quantto provides businesses with the tools they need to manage their inventory efficiently and effectively.
